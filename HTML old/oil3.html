About Our Extra Virgin Olive Oil

Our Extra Virgin Olive Oil is produced in Lebanon using traditional methods that have been passed down through generations. The olives are carefully handpicked at the optimal ripeness and cold-pressed within 24 hours to ensure the highest quality oil.

This premium olive oil is characterized by its golden-green color, fruity aroma, and balanced flavor profile with notes of fresh herbs and a pleasant peppery finish that tingles at the back of your throat – a sign of high-quality olive oil rich in polyphenols.

Our olive oil is unfiltered and contains no additives or preservatives, making it a 100% natural product that retains all its nutritional benefits.

Product Specifications:

  • Origin: Lebanon
  • Acidity: ≤ 0.8%
  • Harvest: October-November
  • Process: Cold extraction
  • Certification: 100% Extra Virgin Olive Oil

Usage Tips

Our Extra Virgin Olive Oil is versatile and can be used in various ways:

Culinary Uses:

  • Dressing: Perfect for salads, drizzle over fresh vegetables or use in vinaigrettes
  • Dipping: Serve with fresh bread as an appetizer
  • Finishing: Drizzle over cooked dishes, soups, or grilled vegetables
  • Cooking: Ideal for low to medium-heat cooking (up to 210°C/410°F)
  • Marinades: Use as a base for meat or vegetable marinades

Traditional Lebanese Recipes:

  • Zaatar & Olive Oil: Mix with zaatar spice blend for a traditional Lebanese dip
  • Hummus: Drizzle over homemade hummus for authentic flavor
  • Tabbouleh: Use in this classic Lebanese salad
  • Baba Ganoush: Essential ingredient in this eggplant dip

Storage Tips:

To preserve the quality and flavor of your olive oil:

  • Store in a cool, dark place away from direct sunlight
  • Keep bottle tightly sealed when not in use
  • Optimal storage temperature: 14-18°C (57-65°F)
  • Best used within 12-18 months of opening
