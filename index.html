About Us

ORSA GROUP is a leading importer and distributor of premium Lebanese products in Europe. We specialize in bringing authentic Lebanese flavors directly from local farmers and producers to your table.

Our mission is to share the rich culinary heritage of Lebanon with the world, offering only the highest quality olive oils, tahini, olives, and other traditional Lebanese delicacies.

With branches in both Belgium and Lebanon, we ensure direct sourcing and quality control at every step of the process, from farm to table.

We take pride in supporting local Lebanese farmers and producers, ensuring fair trade practices and sustainable farming methods.
